## Problem

Collecting text while reading forces constant window switching. You copy something from a PDF or browser, jump to your notes app, paste it, then jump back and try to find your place again. After a few cycles, the switching becomes the slowest part of the process. It breaks your rhythm and adds overhead to something that should be quick.

## What OneKeyCapture Fixes

The tool removes the back‑and‑forth. You stay in the window you're reading. Highlight text, press one key, and the text is captured in the background. No app switching, no losing your place, no stopping to manage the clipboard.

It turns a multi‑step interruption into a single action.

## Impact

- Cuts out the constant window swapping  
- Keeps attention on the page instead of the clipboard  
- Reduces the mental overhead of managing multiple apps  
- Speeds up repetitive capture work  
- Makes text collection part of the reading flow instead of a detour
